BIAKIN THAR HAWNNA : 10th January, 2010 @ 1:00 PM
HAWNGTU : Rev Zosangliana Pakhuangte. Moderator Serlui Presbytery

Kolasib Vengthar Kohhran Committee Dt 26 January 2008 a committee chuan Kum 2008 chhunga hmalakna turin Khuangpuilam Biak in sak nise tih a rel a. Hemi chungchanga hma la a Conveners turin Pastor Lalparchhuana leh Upa H Pazawna te ruat an ni.

Dt 11 February 2008 Vengthar Kohhran committee ah hetiang hian ngaihtuah leh a ni. Committee hmasa in Khuangpuilam ah Biak in sak nise , a mawp phur turin Pastor leh Kohhran Secretary a ruat angina an hmalakna report leh thlan chungchanga lehkha kan hmuh kan chhiarchhuak . Kan sawi hova Biak in hmun hi a hmasa berin Bulldozer in laihzawl phawt nise tih a ni a. Pu R Sengzika hnen atanga thlan chungchang a thu thlen leh Pu C Lallawmzuala hnen atanga Bung thing kung kih chungchanga thu thlente ngaihtuah a ni a. Thlan lungte hi laihzawl hunah a tul angina phun that nise tih a ni a. Biakin sak dan tur hi chu kokim zawka la ngaihtuah leh nise tih a ni.

Dt 15 March 2008 committee ah hetiang hian rel leh a ni a. Sub committee din se tih a nih angin Pastor Lalparchhuana leh Upa H Pazawna te kaihhruaina in din nise.

Dt 31 May 2008 committee ah Biak in hmun laihzawl anih thu dawn a ni a. Bung zar thenkhat thlak tawh anih thu dawn a ni a. Building sub committee din nise tih a ni a, hetiang hian din ani :
Chairman : Upa H Pazawna

Secretary : Pu H Vanlalrema
Asst Secretary : Pu Lallianthanga

Fin Secretary : Pu PC Hmingthanga
Work Superviser : Pu R Sangliana

Lungphun te hi a ngai anga phun lovin Lungremin Granite ah Mitthi hmingte ziak tlarin PHE Tuizem bulah phun nise tih a ni a. Biak in hi a dung 50ft ( Vestry chhung thlakin ) , avang 30ft a sak nise tih a ni a. Biak in Skirting thleng Ita ( Brick ) rem nise, a chung Rangva , a bang Tile nise ti a rel a ni a. Hmalakna atan Rs 100000.00 ( Nuaikhat ) hman nise, Builsing sum atangin Rs 50000.00 leh chhawmdawlna sum atangin Rs 50000.00 nise.Sumte hi Synod Financial Rule angina enlawl nise, Audit theih tura vawn tura tih a ni. Dt 27 September 2008 Committee ah Biak in Lungphum nise tih a ni a. Hetiang hian ruahmanna siam a ni :
A Hun : Dt 18 October 2008. Inrinni zing dar 7 :00
Phumtu : Pastor Lalparchhuana, Bialtu Pastor
Chairman : Upa RK Zamlova. Committee Chairman.

Lungphum tur ngaihtuah turin Kohhran Secretary Upa H Pazawna leh Tresurer Upa R Sangthuama te ruat an ni a. Bial chhung Kohhran committee member te sawm nise, thingpui leh a hmeh buatsaih nise tih a ni a. Kohhran Zaipawl te zai tura tih a ni.Thlalak ho nise tih a ni bawk. Dt 25 October Committee ah Biak in Pulpit tur sawi a ni a. Pi Zodinpuii w/o Pu H Lalthianghlima ten Rs 30000.00 ( singthum ) an pe a. Building sub committee in Biak in sak chhunzawm na atan Rs 116000.00 ( Nuaikhat singkhat sangruk ) an dill eh a, an dil ang hian Kohhran dinnghehna sum Synod atanga dawn Rs 30000.00, Tualchhung sum Rs 46000.00 leh Chhawmdawlna sum atangin Rs 40000.00 chu Building sub committee ah pek nise tih a rel a ni. Dt 27 November 2008 committee ah Biak in sakna atan a sum hmanral tawh zawng zawng chu Rs 227670.00 ( nuai hnih singhnih sansarih zaruk sawmsarih ) anih thu Building Secretary Pu Lallianthangan a report. Dt 20 December 2008 committee ah Khuangpuilam Biak in Building Sub committee hming puttir nise tih a ni a. Hruaitu atan hetiang hian ruahmanna siam a ni :

Dt 31 January 2009 committee ah Khuangpuilam Biak in sak pual Special iop siam nise tih a ni a.Tumkhat atan tih ni phaet se tih a ni. Biak in chhuat chhun hnatlang neih nise tih a n I a, a hun atan 14 February 2009 ruat a ni. Special ip atanga sum lut Rs 50625.00 chu Biak in sak nan sanction a ni.
