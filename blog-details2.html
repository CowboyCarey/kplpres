Dt 11 February 2008 Vengthar Kohhran committee ah hetiang hian ngaihtuah leh a ni. Dt 15 March 2008 committee ah hetiang hian rel leh a ni a. Sub committee din se tih a nih angin Pastor Lalparchhuana leh Upa H Pazawna te kaihhruaina in din nise.
